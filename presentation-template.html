A Jewelry Repair Intake App

Zach Parris aka "Zach The Jeweler"

July 9th, 2016

Project Goals:

  • Jewelry can be a huge investment! You wouldn't want your mechanic to be in a rush to get you out the door. To ensure an above standard customer experience, I created an app that expedites the repair intake process for jewelry stores.

  • This will ensure a better customer experience, and a thorough inspection.

  • Version 2 will have a price sheet, and price generator, as well as a calendar to see upcoming work orders.
